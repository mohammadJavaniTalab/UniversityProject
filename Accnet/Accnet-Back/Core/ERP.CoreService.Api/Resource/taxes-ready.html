To have AccNet Online e-file your taxes with CRA:
1. Login at https://www.accnetonline.com
2. Click the arrow beside your user name.
3. Click Sign Documents.
4. Download and print your tax return for your records.
5. Click I want AccNet to E-File.
6. Repeat steps 3, 4 and 5 for any spouse or dependents on your profile.
7. Once complete, AccNet Online will e-file the return(s) with the CRA.
To file your own taxes with the CRA;
1. Login at https://www.accnetonline.com
2. Click the arrow beside your user name.
3. Click Sign Documents.
4. Download and print your tax return for your records.
5. Click I do my own filing.
6. Log Out.
7. File your own taxes with the CRA.
